The Warden

Warden is an extremely powerful hostile mob summoned by sculk shriekers in the deep dark biome. It attacks by swinging its arms downward, dealing the strongest melee damage of all mobs, and can also release a sonic boom attack that immediately strikes the target, piercing through obstacles and deals damage that cannot be reduced except by the Resistance effect.

Enderman

The Enderman is a tall neutral mob found in all three dimensions. Endermen normally ignore players and lazily amble about, but they will swiftly walk to attack those that damage it or look directly at its face.

Enderman Wiki
Ghast

Ghasts are large white floating jellyfish ghosts. They are hostile mobs that live in the Nether and shoot explosive fireballs at the player.
